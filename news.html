Liverpool Are a Few Games Away From Winning the Title

As the 2024/25 season enters its decisive final stretch, Liverpool find themselves sitting eleven points clear of second-placed Arsenal with a handful of matches remaining. The Reds have been imperious all campaign — grinding out results when it mattered and blowing teams away when they clicked into gear.

The mathematics are simple: win their next two fixtures and the title is theirs, regardless of what anyone else does. Their squad is fresh, their confidence is sky-high, and Anfield will be rocking for what could be a historic night.

Arsenal, for their part, have refused to give up. The Gunners continue to pick up points and hope Liverpool slip up — but with this kind of lead and this kind of form, that is looking increasingly unlikely. The title is all but booked for Merseyside.
