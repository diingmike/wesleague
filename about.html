About

Nairobi, Kenya  ·  6-a-side Football

Where the
Game Lives.

Wes League is Nairobi's premier 6-a-side football community — built by players, for players. Fast football. Fierce competition. Real community.

Our Story

Wes League was born out of a simple idea: great football shouldn't require eleven players, a full-sized pitch, or a mountain of admin. We wanted something fast, competitive, and easy to organise — so we built it ourselves, right here in Nairobi.

6-a-side is a different kind of football. Every player touches the ball more, every goal matters more, and every game is over in a flash. It demands sharp instincts, tight teamwork, and a hunger for the next match. That energy is what Wes League is built on.

This platform exists to keep everything in one place — fixtures, results, standings, stats, and the stories behind every gameweek. No group chats, no spreadsheets, no confusion. Just football.

Whether you're chasing the title or just here for the love of the game — welcome to Wes League.

How It Works
Location Based in Nairobi, Kenya. All fixtures are played at agreed local venues.
Format Every team plays each other twice — home and away — across two full legs.
Officials Referees and stat keepers are provided by the league for every fixture.
Champion The team with the most points after the second leg is crowned champion.
Tiebreaker If teams are level on points, goal difference determines the winner.
